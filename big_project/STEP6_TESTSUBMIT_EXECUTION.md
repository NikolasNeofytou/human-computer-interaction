## üîó GET THE TWO REQUIRED LINKS (5 minutes)
You must submit two separate links to Helios: (A) View/Share link and (B) Invite/Edit link.

### A ‚Äî Share Link (View access)
1. Click **Share** (top-right of Figma)
2. Under link settings, set **"Anyone with the link"** ‚Üí **Can view**
3. Click **Copy link**
4. Paste into a safe place (e.g., Notepad) ‚Äî this will be the first line of your Helios submission

### B ‚Äî Invite Link (Edit access)
There are two methods depending on your team settings and admin privileges.

Method 1 (Preferred - Team Admin):
1. Click **Share**
2. Under "Invite to file" ‚Üí click the dropdown near the email field
3. Change permission to **"Anyone with the link can edit"**
4. Click **Copy invite link**
5. Paste into Notepad ‚Äî this will be the second line of your Helios submission

Method 2 (If the previous option is disabled):
1. In the team settings (left sidebar), invite a placeholder email (your second email or friend's) with edit access and copy the invitation link.
2. Alternatively, invite graders directly by email if required by Helios.

**Important**: Confirm the invite link grants edit access in an incognito/private browser session (see test below)
